Parallella

Expansion Board

This project template may be used as the basis for expansion boards for the Parallella PCB.

This project includes a schematic defining all IO from the Parallella PCB. The user may select any or all of the connectors depending on need for the application, simply by including or deleting the associated schematic symbol at the top level. Also included is a PCB of the same size as the Parallella PCB with connectors placed correctly to align the two boards. All IO and power rails present on the Parallella are connected to the daughtercard through the 0.5mm Samtec BSH expansion headers.
